The Finishing Touch Medspa

Driven by his passion for helping people look and feel great, Dr. John Hernried founded The Finishing Touch Medspa in 2018. After the success of the Hernried Center for Medical Weight Loss, he wanted to focus on addressing the unwanted changes that occur after losing weight. Often, patients experience side effects that prevent them from achieving their body goals such as loose skin, wrinkles and stubborn areas of fat that don’t respond to diet and exercise. Our dedicated team at The Finishing Touch Medspa can help you love what you see in the mirror by targeting these problem areas. Our philosophy is simple: looking beautiful should be easy at any age, and it’s our goal to make that happen!
