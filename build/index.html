The Finishing Touch Medspa

Driven by his passion for helping people look and feel great, Dr. John Hernried founded The Finishing Touch Medspa in 2018. After the success of the Hernried Center for Medical Weight Loss, he wanted to focus on addressing the unwanted changes that occur after losing weight. Often, patients experience side effects that prevent them from achieving their body goals such as loose skin, wrinkles and stubborn areas of fat that don’t respond to diet and exercise. Our dedicated team at The Finishing Touch Medspa can help you love what you see in the mirror by targeting these problem areas. Our philosophy is simple: looking beautiful should be easy at any age, and it’s our goal to make that happen!

Why The Finishing Touch Medspa is The Right Choice For You

Dr. John Hernried has been helping patients feel better and look great since 1983. His approach has helped thousands of Hernried Center for Medical Weight Loss patients rediscover their future and leave with safe and satisfying results. At The Finishing Touch Medspa, you can experience the same level of dedicated, outstanding service. Our decades of experience in helping patients overcome weight loss has made us experts in targeting problem areas and the undesired effects of losing weight. We offer effective, nonsurgical treatment options that are designed to address these issues and our experienced care providers are among the most committed in the country. We’ve helped thousands of patients lose weight, get healthy and feel great on the inside — now let us help you transform on the outside with a look that reveals your new inner confidence.
